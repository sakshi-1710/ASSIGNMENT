My Fav Hobby

Why I Like It

My favourite hobby is listening to music. I enjoy music because it makes me feel relaxed and happy. Music helps reduce my stress after a long day and improves my mood. I like different types of songs such as pop, classical, and soft music because they refresh my mind and give me positive energy.

How I Spend Time Doing It

I usually listen to music in the evening or during my free time. Sometimes I listen to songs while studying or traveling. On weekends, I create playlists of my favourite songs and spend time enjoying them. Listening to music helps me stay calm and focused.
